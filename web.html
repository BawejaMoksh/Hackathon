Product

Our product is suitable for remote location data monitoring and is very cheap to produce. It collects data from the Earth Moving Machines and transmits them to LoRa Receiver without the requirement of any network coverage or bluetooth or wifi.After receiving the data transmitted through LoRa in our arduino UNO, we log that data in an excel file through a software named PLX-DAQ. These excel sheets will be later uploaded to our web-app which can be accessed by the engineers/owner to monitor data and help with the fleet management. When network coverage is available to our Website/Application that we will develop for login users to see, data will be accessible directly. We have streamlined the process of acquiring the data and brought it to your fingertips using a reliable and safe technology which does not require wifi/mobile data coverage.
